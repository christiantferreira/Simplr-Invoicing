Privacy Policy — Simplr Invoicing

Effective date: June 6, 2025

Simplr Invoicing ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Simplr Invoicing application (the "Service").

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our Service, including integration with your Google account and Gmail data.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our Service and authorize Google OAuth access, we may collect and process the following information:

  • Basic profile information (name, email address) provided by Google OAuth.
  • Access tokens and refresh tokens required to securely access your Gmail account.
  • Email metadata and content only as required to enable the app’s functionality (sending invoices via Gmail and reading relevant invoice replies).
  • Technical and usage data related to app performance.

We do not collect or process Gmail data beyond what is strictly necessary for the app’s functionality. We do not access or store your emails unless required to process an action requested by you (such as sending an invoice email or retrieving its status).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for the following purposes:

  • To allow you to authenticate and use the Simplr Invoicing app.
  • To enable functionality such as sending invoices via your Gmail account.
  • To maintain app performance and security.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Your tokens (access and refresh tokens) are securely stored in our backend (Supabase) using industry best practices. Access to these tokens is restricted to authorized processes required to operate the app.

We apply appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at:

admin@simplraccounting.com

Upon receiving such a request, we will promptly delete your data from our systems.

5. Google API Services User Data Policy Compliance

Simplr Invoicing’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

7. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
